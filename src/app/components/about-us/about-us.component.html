A project for the automation and implementation of NFC in everyday life

In 2021, in the midst of a major financial crisis, “Chips” was born, a company founded with a clear mission: to automate tasks using NFC technology. What started as an innovative project soon faced challenges. Over the years, there were moments of success and moments of difficulty, with breaks in production due to the team being dissatisfied with the quality of their products. However, Chips' vision was never to “sell for the sake of selling”. Every step taken was aimed at offering more than just a product: an experience. Today, Chips has established itself as a brand that offers quality, style and the opportunity to customize your personal brand. Its slogan says it all: “Our way of sharing”.
